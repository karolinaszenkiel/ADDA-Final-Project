How Do I is a site designed to alleviate the small panics of everyday life. If you've ever wondered whether to sit in the front or back seat of an Uber, which line to stand in at confusing DIY restaurant, or whether or not to talk to your stylist while you get a haircut - this website is for you.

Just search up what you're trying to do and you'll find instructions and reviews from other How Do I users! You'll find ratings on how busy a location is, how loud a location is, or how easy to navigate the situation will be, along with helpful hints and tips from other users.

Been someplace confusing and want to leave a helpful review for others? Feel free to submit an instruction or review by hitting the "submit" button above!
