About

How Do I is a site designed to alleviate the small panics of everyday life. If you've ever wondered whether to sit in the front or back seat of an Uber, which line to stand in at confusing DIY salad bowl restaurant, or whether or not to talk to your stylist while you get a haircut - this website is for you.
